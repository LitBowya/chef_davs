Our Journey

From Passion to Perfection

Chef Davs began as a dream shared between two culinary enthusiasts in a small kitchen in New York City. What started as intimate dinner parties for friends has blossomed into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our founder, Chef David Mensah, trained in the finest kitchens of Paris and Tokyo before returning home with a vision: to create a restaurant that celebrates the art of food while honoring the warmth of home cooking.

Every dish we serve carries the essence of that original dream ‚Äî crafted with love, presented with artistry, and delivered with genuine hospitality. We believe that dining is not just about eating; it's about creating memories that last a lifetime.
